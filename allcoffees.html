Batch Brew

Also listed as drip or house coffee, batch brew coffee is brewed in a large carafe and served instantly. The majority of coffee shops have a light, medium, or dark roast option on tap. Decaf is also offered if you need a break from caffeine.

$10

Pour over coffee

Pour over coffee is brewed by the cup using a specific method such as a Kalita Wave or Chemex. Some specialty coffee shops have a full array of pour over methods to choose from including French Press, Hario V60, or siphon pot. This coffee will take about 5-10 minutes to make since this method is brewed by hand.

$20
